About

Mirage Laser Designs is an owner-operated company located in Lethbridge, Alberta. It was established back in 2004 by Steve Kothig who is the current owner of Mirage Laser Designs. The Mirage Laser Designs location features a retail showroom and a custom workshop on-site to help meet our customers needs and provide faster results for smaller orders. We work closely with our communities and their local clubs. Over the years we have built relationships with Southern Alberta 4-H clubs, various sports teams, municipal government and non-profit organizations. We have become well known for our creativity and delivery of high-quality products that always exceed expectations of our customers.



Our business is all about making things personal. We use the newest technology combined with creativity and your own inspiration to create unique products that represent you or your business. Because we are a small company, the owner works personally with each customer to ensure that you receive the highest quality product.
