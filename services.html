Graphic Design

Graphic designers create visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for various applications such as advertisements, brochures, magazines, and corporate reports.

Laser Etching

Laser Engraving (or Laser Etching) is a Subtractive Manufacturing method, that uses a laser beam to change the surface of an object. This process is mostly used to create images on the material. To do so, the laser creates high heat that will vaporize the matter, thus exposing cavities that will form the final image.

Color Imprinting

Color imprinting is the process of loading colored ink into the printer and that is pressed along the surface of the screen or pad to then be transferred to your promo item. Color imprinting can be done with any design you send to us that is pressed and transferred onto a variety of materials .

Sand Etching

A stencil is produced from artwork and applied to glass, crystal, marble, slate, and wood. This item is then placed in a sandblasting booth where tiny grains of sand and high-pressure compressed air is forced through an airbrush and directed at the applied stencil. The sand blasts away at the open areas of the stencil leaving behind your engraved design.

Temp. Tattoos

A decorative image that can be applied to the skin for short periods of time. Most temporary tattoos are novelty items made with a special type of decal. A process known as screen printing is used to create the tattoo image on paper coated with a transfer film. We can take any custom designs and make as many tattoos as requested.

Weddings

A wedding/engagement service which can provide your wedding or engagement party with custom engraved or color imprinted items such as: wine glasses, cloth napkins, candle holders, seating cards, posters, woodworkings, and any other custom designed items that are needed to make your wedding, the most memorable day possible.
